# Calculator Lab - Calculator Descriptions

**Website:** https://calculatorlab.org

This document provides factual descriptions of the calculators available on Calculator Lab.

---

## What Calculator Lab Offers

Calculator Lab is a free online calculator website with 225+ calculators across four categories. All calculators run in the browser, require no downloads or registration, and do not store user data on servers.

---

## Math Calculators (52 tools)

### Basic & Scientific

**Basic Calculator**
Performs standard arithmetic: addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Includes clear, backspace, and equals functions.

**Scientific Calculator**
Extends basic arithmetic with:
- Trigonometric functions (sin, cos, tan) with degree/radian toggle
- Inverse trigonometric functions
- Logarithms (log10, natural log)
- Exponents and roots
- Memory functions (M+, M-, MR, MC)
- Constants (pi, e)

### Algebra

**Quadratic Formula Calculator**
Solves equations in the form ax² + bx + c = 0. Returns real or complex roots with step-by-step solutions showing the discriminant calculation.

**Slope Calculator**
Calculates slope (m) from two coordinate points. Also provides the y-intercept and line equation in slope-intercept form (y = mx + b).

**Exponent Calculator**
Computes powers including positive, negative, and fractional exponents. Handles large number calculations.

### Fractions & Percentages

**Fraction Calculator**
Adds, subtracts, multiplies, and divides fractions. Shows simplified results and can handle mixed numbers.

**Percentage Calculator**
Three modes:
1. Find X% of a number
2. Find what percentage A is of B
3. Calculate percentage increase/decrease

**Decimal to Fraction Calculator**
Converts decimal numbers to fractions in lowest terms.

### Geometry

**Triangle Calculator**
Given known values (sides, angles), calculates unknown sides, angles, area, and perimeter. Supports right, isosceles, equilateral, and scalene triangles.

**Area Calculator**
Computes area for: rectangles, squares, circles, triangles, trapezoids, parallelograms, and ellipses.

**Volume Calculator**
Computes volume for 3D shapes: cubes, rectangular prisms, spheres, cylinders, cones, and pyramids.

**Perimeter Calculator**
Calculates perimeter for common 2D shapes.

**Circle Calculator**
From radius, diameter, circumference, or area, calculates all other circle properties.

**Pythagorean Theorem Calculator**
Finds the missing side of a right triangle given two sides. Shows the formula and calculation.

### Statistics

**Standard Deviation Calculator**
Accepts a data set and calculates:
- Mean (average)
- Population and sample standard deviation
- Variance
- Sum of values
- Count

**Mean Median Mode Calculator**
Calculates all three measures of central tendency from a data set.

**Probability Calculator**
Computes probability for single events, combined events (AND/OR), and conditional probability.

**Z-Score Calculator**
Calculates z-scores for standardizing data points. Shows the standard normal distribution context.

**Sample Size Calculator**
Determines required sample size for surveys given confidence level, margin of error, and population size.

### Number Systems

**Binary Calculator**
Performs arithmetic in binary (base-2). Converts between binary, decimal, octal, and hexadecimal.

**Hex Calculator**
Performs hexadecimal arithmetic and conversions to other number systems.

### Other Math Tools

**Random Number Generator**
Generates random integers within a specified range. Can generate single numbers or sets.

**Prime Factorization Calculator**
Breaks down any integer into its prime factors.

**GCF Calculator**
Finds the greatest common factor of two or more numbers.

**LCM Calculator**
Finds the least common multiple of two or more numbers.

**Factor Calculator**
Lists all factors of a given integer.

## Financial Calculators (74 tools)

### Loans & Mortgages

**Mortgage Calculator**
Inputs: home price, down payment (amount or percentage), interest rate, loan term, property tax, home insurance, PMI, HOA fees.
Outputs: monthly payment breakdown, total payment over loan life, total interest paid.
Formula: M = P × [r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n - 1]

**Loan Calculator**
General-purpose loan calculator for any loan type. Inputs: principal, interest rate, term. Outputs: monthly payment, total interest, amortization schedule.

**Auto Loan Calculator**
Loan calculator pre-configured for vehicle purchases. Includes trade-in value and sales tax options.

**Personal Loan Calculator**
Calculates payments for unsecured personal loans.

**Student Loan Calculator**
Handles multiple repayment plans including standard, graduated, and income-driven options.

**Mortgage Payoff Calculator**
Shows how extra payments reduce loan term and total interest.

**Refinance Calculator**
Compares current mortgage to refinance options. Calculates break-even point and savings.

**FHA Loan Calculator**
Includes FHA-specific requirements: 3.5% minimum down payment, upfront and annual MIP.

**VA Mortgage Calculator**
Accounts for VA loan features: no PMI, VA funding fee options.

### Interest & Investments

**Compound Interest Calculator**
Formula: A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt)
Inputs: principal, rate, time, compounding frequency (annual, semi-annual, quarterly, monthly, daily).
Outputs: future value, total interest earned.

**Simple Interest Calculator**
Formula: I = P × r × t
Calculates interest without compounding.

**Investment Calculator**
Projects investment growth with regular contributions. Accounts for compound interest and contribution timing.

**ROI Calculator**
Calculates return on investment as a percentage: ROI = (Gain - Cost) / Cost × 100

**Future Value Calculator**
Determines future value of current investment at a given rate.

**Present Value Calculator**
Determines current value of a future sum discounted at a given rate.

**IRR Calculator**
Calculates internal rate of return for a series of cash flows.

**CD Calculator**
Calculates certificate of deposit maturity value based on APY and term.

### Retirement

**Retirement Calculator**
Inputs: current age, retirement age, current savings, monthly contribution, expected return.
Outputs: projected retirement balance, monthly retirement income estimate.

**401K Calculator**
Factors in employer matching, contribution limits, and tax-deferred growth.

**IRA Calculator**
Projects traditional or Roth IRA growth with annual contribution limits.

**Roth IRA Calculator**
Specifically models Roth IRA with after-tax contributions and tax-free growth.

**RMD Calculator**
Calculates required minimum distributions based on IRS life expectancy tables.

**Pension Calculator**
Estimates pension benefits based on years of service and final average salary.

**Social Security Calculator**
Estimates Social Security benefits based on earnings history and claiming age.

### Taxes

**Tax Calculator**
Estimates federal income tax based on filing status, income, and deductions. Uses current tax brackets.

**Income Tax Calculator**
Detailed income tax estimation with itemized vs. standard deduction comparison.

**Sales Tax Calculator**
Calculates sales tax and total price from pre-tax amount and tax rate.

**Paycheck Calculator**
Estimates take-home pay after federal, state, and FICA taxes.

**Salary Calculator**
Converts between hourly, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, and annual salary.

### Debt

**Credit Card Payoff Calculator**
Shows time to pay off credit card balance with minimum or fixed payments.

**Debt Payoff Calculator**
Compares debt payoff strategies: avalanche (highest rate first) vs. snowball (lowest balance first).

**Debt Consolidation Calculator**
Compares current debts to a consolidated loan option.

**Debt to Income Calculator**
Calculates DTI ratio used by lenders.

### Other Financial

**Budget Calculator**
Categorizes income and expenses, shows net income and savings rate.

**Tip Calculator**
Calculates tip amount and splits bill among multiple people.

**Inflation Calculator**
Adjusts dollar amounts for inflation between years using CPI data.

**Currency Calculator**
Converts between currencies (note: rates require external data source).

## Health & Fitness Calculators (36 tools)

### Body Metrics

**BMI Calculator**
Formula (metric): BMI = weight(kg) / height(m)²
Supports metric and imperial units. Displays BMI category (underweight, normal, overweight, obese I/II/III) based on WHO classifications.

**Body Fat Calculator**
Estimates body fat percentage using various methods:
- Navy method (circumference measurements)
- Skinfold methods
Separate calculations for men and women.

**Ideal Weight Calculator**
Calculates ideal weight ranges using multiple formulas:
- Devine formula
- Robinson formula
- Miller formula
- Hamwi formula

**BMR Calculator**
Calculates basal metabolic rate using:
- Mifflin-St Jeor equation (recommended)
- Harris-Benedict equation
Factors: age, gender, height, weight.

**TDEE Calculator**
Total Daily Energy Expenditure = BMR × Activity Multiplier
Activity levels: sedentary, lightly active, moderately active, very active, extra active.

**Lean Body Mass Calculator**
Estimates fat-free mass from total weight and body fat percentage.

### Nutrition

**Calorie Calculator**
Determines daily calorie needs for:
- Weight maintenance
- Weight loss (deficit)
- Weight gain (surplus)
Based on TDEE calculation.

**Macro Calculator**
Distributes daily calories into:
- Protein (grams)
- Carbohydrates (grams)
- Fat (grams)
Supports common ratios (balanced, low-carb, high-protein, keto).

**Protein Calculator**
Recommends daily protein intake based on weight and activity level.

**Carbohydrate Calculator**
Calculates carbohydrate needs based on calorie target and diet type.

**Fat Intake Calculator**
Calculates fat gram requirements based on calorie target.

**Water Intake Calculator**
Estimates daily water needs based on weight and activity level.

### Fitness

**Calories Burned Calculator**
Estimates calories burned for various activities using MET values.

**One Rep Max Calculator**
Estimates 1RM from weight lifted and reps performed. Uses Epley, Brzycki, and other formulas.

**Pace Calculator**
Converts between pace (min/mile or min/km) and speed. Calculates finish time for given distance.

**Marathon Pace Calculator**
Calculates pace needed to achieve target marathon finish time.

**Heart Rate Zone Calculator**
Determines training zones based on maximum heart rate or heart rate reserve (Karvonen formula).

**Target Heart Rate Calculator**
Calculates target heart rate ranges for exercise intensity.

**VO2 Max Calculator**
Estimates maximal oxygen uptake from various test results.

### Pregnancy

**Due Date Calculator**
Calculates estimated due date from last menstrual period (LMP) or conception date. Uses standard 280-day (40-week) gestation.

**Pregnancy Calculator**
Shows current pregnancy week and trimester from due date or LMP.

**Ovulation Calculator**
Estimates ovulation date and fertile window based on cycle length.

**Conception Calculator**
Back-calculates likely conception date from due date or birth date.

**Pregnancy Weight Gain Calculator**
Recommends weight gain based on pre-pregnancy BMI using IOM guidelines.

## Technical Notes

### Calculation Accuracy

- All calculators perform calculations client-side in JavaScript
- Floating-point arithmetic is used, which may introduce minor rounding differences in some cases
- Financial calculators round to 2 decimal places for currency display
- Formulas are documented on each calculator page

### Unit Support

Many calculators support both:
- **Metric:** kilograms, meters, Celsius
- **Imperial:** pounds, feet/inches, Fahrenheit

Users can toggle between systems where applicable.

### Browser Compatibility

Calculators run in modern browsers supporting ES6+ JavaScript:
- Chrome (recent versions)
- Firefox (recent versions)
- Safari (recent versions)
- Edge (recent versions)

### Data Privacy

- Calculations are performed locally in the browser
- No calculation data is transmitted to or stored on servers
- No user account or registration required
