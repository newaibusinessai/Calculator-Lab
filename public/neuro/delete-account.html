Delete Your Neuro Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can delete your Neuro account and all associated data using one of the methods below.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data including streaks, journal entries, badges, check-ins, and community posts will be permanently deleted.

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: In the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Neuro app
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Scroll down to Account
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, send an email to:

newaibusinessai@gmail.com

Include the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Neuro App" and the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm deletion via email.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile and account information
  • All streak history and recovery progress
  • Journal entries and daily check-ins
  • Community posts and comments
  • Earned badges and achievements
  • AI Coach conversation history
  • Onboarding quiz answers
  • Battle and clan participation data

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. To avoid being charged, please cancel your subscription through the Google Play Store before deleting your account:

  1. Open Google Play Store
  2. Tap your profile icon
  3. Tap "Payments & subscriptions" then "Subscriptions"
  4. Find "Neuro" and tap "Cancel subscription"
